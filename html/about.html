About

This short documentary will provide a glimpse of history in regard to the Sikh Genocide. Not many people are aware of what happened in 1984 and how the genocide still continues until this day. Despite being credited as the largest democracy of the world, Indian government tends to keep their dirty secrets under wraps. The assimilation and mass killings/injustices against Sikhs in India is what I want to shine light on.
